## About the role

KPMG Global Services is hiring a Business Associate for its Quality and Risk Management team in Gurugram. This role offers an opportunity to work with international teams on risk assessment, due diligence, and compliance-related projects. Ideal for fresh graduates and early-career professionals, the position involves conducting research, analysing screening results, managing stakeholder relationships, and supporting risk mitigation initiatives. Candidates with strong analytical abilities, attention to detail, and an interest in compliance and risk management will gain valuable exposure to global business operations in a fast-paced professional services environment.

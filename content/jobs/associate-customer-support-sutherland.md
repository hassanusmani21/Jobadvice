## About the role

Sutherland is hiring Associates for its customer support team in Bhopal. The position offers an opportunity to develop communication, customer service, and operational skills in a fast-paced environment while working with a global digital transformation company. Additional benefits include cab facilities for employees residing within 25 km of the office location.

## About the role

NatWest Group is hiring Customer Service & Operations Analysts for its Gurugram hub. This entry-level opportunity is designed for recent graduates who want to build a career in banking operations and customer service. The role involves supporting contact centre operations, resolving customer queries, analysing performance data, and contributing to process improvements. Successful candidates will work in a collaborative environment focused on operational excellence, customer satisfaction, and continuous improvement while developing valuable analytical and communication skills.

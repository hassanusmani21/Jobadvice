## About the role

Apple's Marcom Quality Engineering team is hiring an SDET Engineering Manager to lead a Hyderabad-based team responsible for building automation frameworks, test platforms, and internal quality engineering tools. The role involves managing engineers and vendor partners, driving automation strategy, improving CI/CD quality signals, and collaborating closely with engineering and QE leadership in California. The position is focused on scaling quality engineering practices, improving automation reliability, and enabling efficient cross-geo software delivery across Apple's marketing technology ecosystem.

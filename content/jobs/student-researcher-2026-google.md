Google's Student Researcher, 2026 opening is an India-based internship/apprentice opportunity for students who want direct research exposure inside Google teams. The official Google Careers page says applicants should complete the application before July 31, 2026 and be located in India for the duration of the engagement. Candidates should prepare an updated resume and an unofficial or official transcript in English before starting the application.

This role is best suited for students who can connect academic work with practical research areas such as machine learning, natural language understanding, computer vision, optimization, data science, software engineering, or related computing fields. Google notes that Student Researcher projects are exploratory and can vary by team, duration, and location. Before applying, candidates should review the official page carefully, confirm the current deadline, and make sure their resume highlights research projects, programming experience, publications, coursework, and any collaborative technical work.

For a stronger application, students should avoid generic resumes. Add a focused research summary, list relevant tools and programming languages, and describe one or two projects where you solved a clear technical problem. If you have papers, posters, thesis work, open-source contributions, or lab experience, include them clearly with links where possible.

## What is TCS NQT?

TCS NQT (National Qualifier Test) is a standardized assessment conducted by TCS to evaluate candidates in:

- Numerical Ability
- Reasoning Ability
- Verbal Ability
- Programming Skills

Based on your performance, you can be shortlisted under Ninja, Digital, or Prime categories.

---

## TCS NQT Selection Process

1. Online Written Test (Cognitive + Programming)
2. Technical Interview
3. Managerial Interview
4. HR Interview

Strong written test performance improves your chances of qualifying for the higher package categories.

---

## TCS NQT Exam Pattern 2026

| Section | Topics Covered | Difficulty |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Numerical Ability | Percentages, Profit and Loss, Time and Work, Data Interpretation | Moderate |
| Reasoning | Puzzles, Coding-Decoding, Analytical Reasoning | Moderate |
| Verbal | Reading Comprehension, Vocabulary, Grammar | Easy-Moderate |
| Programming | DSA, Problem Solving, Algorithms | Moderate-High |

Programming performance plays a major role in salary category selection.

## Interview Preparation Tips

### Technical Round

- Explain your logic clearly
- Write structured and readable code
- Revise OOPS, DBMS, and core CS fundamentals

### HR Round

- Prepare answers for common HR questions
- Show confidence, flexibility, and ownership
- Maintain a positive and professional attitude

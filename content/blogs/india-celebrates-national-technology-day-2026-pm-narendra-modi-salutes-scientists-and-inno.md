# India Celebrates National Technology Day 2026: PM Narendra Modi Salutes Scientists and Innovators

India marked National Technology Day 2026 on May 11 with celebrations across the country, recognizing the achievements of scientists, engineers, innovators, and technology leaders who continue shaping the nation’s future.

Prime Minister Narendra Modi extended greetings to citizens and praised India’s scientific community for its contributions toward innovation, self-reliance, and national development.

## Why National Technology Day Is Celebrated

National Technology Day is observed every year on May 11 to commemorate India’s successful Pokhran-II nuclear tests conducted in 1998 under the leadership of former Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayee.

The historic tests, also known as Operation Shakti, marked a defining moment in India’s strategic and technological history. Following the achievement, the Government of India officially declared May 11 as National Technology Day in 1999.

Prime Minister Narendra Modi shared a message honoring India’s scientific excellence and technological achievements.

In his message, the Prime Minister recalled the dedication and hard work of Indian scientists behind the Pokhran tests and highlighted how technology continues to play a major role in building a self-reliant and future-ready India.

## Importance of National Technology Day

National Technology Day is more than a symbolic celebration. It also serves as inspiration for students, engineers, entrepreneurs, and innovators working toward India’s technological future.

The day encourages:

- Scientific research
- Innovation culture
- Technology education
- Startup development
- Indigenous manufacturing
- Digital transformation

Experts believe that sustained investment in research and innovation will play a critical role in India’s ambition to become a global technology powerhouse over the next decade.

\## What is TCS NQT?



TCS NQT (National Qualifier Test) is a standardized assessment conducted by TCS to evaluate candidates in:



\- Numerical Ability  

\- Reasoning Ability  

\- Verbal Ability  

\- Programming Skills  



Based on performance, candidates are shortlisted under Ninja, Digital, or Prime categories.



\---



\## TCS NQT Selection Process



1. Online Written Test (Cognitive + Programming)  

2. Technical Interview  

3. Managerial Interview  

4. HR Interview  



Strong written test scores improve your chances of a higher package.



\---



\## TCS NQT Exam Pattern 2026



\| Section | Topics Covered | Difficulty |

\|----------|----------------|------------|

\| Numerical Ability | Percentages, P&L, Time & Work, DI | Moderate |

\| Reasoning | Puzzles, Coding-Decoding | Moderate |

\| Verbal | Reading Comprehension, Grammar | Easy–Moderate |

\| Programming | DSA, Algorithms | Moderate–High |



Programming performance plays a major role in salary category selection.

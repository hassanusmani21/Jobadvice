# 📉 What Happened in Oracle Layoffs 2026?

In early April 2026, Oracle began large-scale layoffs affecting employees across multiple countries.

Reports suggest:

- Around **20,000 to 30,000 employees were laid off globally**
- Nearly **12,000 employees in India were affected**
- Employees received **termination emails early in the morning**
- Many roles were eliminated as part of restructuring

This makes it one of the **largest layoffs in recent IT history**.

The layoffs happened across departments including:

- Cloud services  
- Sales teams  
- Customer operations  
- Technical roles  

And this is what shocked the industry.

# 😨 Why This News Matters for Freshers

Freshers are already facing challenges.

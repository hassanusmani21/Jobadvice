\## Eligibility Criteria



\### Academic Requirements

\- Minimum 60–70% in previous degree  

\- Recognized board or university  



\### English Language Tests

\- IELTS (6.0–7.5 bands)  

\- TOEFL  

\- PTE  



\### Entrance Exams (If Required)

\- GRE (for MS)  

\- GMAT (for MBA)

\## Student Visa Process



1. Receive admission letter  

2. Pay tuition deposit  

3. Arrange financial documents  

4. Apply for student visa  

5. Attend interview (if required)  

6. Receive visa approval  



\---



\## Common Visa Rejection Reasons



\- Incomplete documentation  

\- Weak financial proof  

\- Poor SOP  

\- Academic gaps without explanation

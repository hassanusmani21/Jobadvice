\# How to Get Internship in 1st Year of College in India



Getting an internship in the 1st year of college may seem difficult. Many students think companies only hire final year students but that is not true.



In 2026 startups and growing companies are actively hiring early learners. If you start in your first year you gain real world exposure practical skills and a strong resume advantage.



This step by step guide explains how first year college students in India can get internships.



\---



\## Why Do Internship in 1st Year



Starting early helps you



\- Gain practical industry exposure  

\- Improve communication skills  

\- Build a strong resume early  

\- Increase chances of better internships later  

\- Improve placement opportunities  



Students who start early usually stay ahead.



\---



\## Step 1 Choose One Skill



Before applying decide what skill you want to build.



Popular beginner friendly skills



\- Digital Marketing  

\- Content Writing  

\- Graphic Design  

\- Video Editing  

\- Web Development  

\- Social Media Management  



Focus on one skill and build basics first.

If web development interests you, use this [web development roadmap for 2026](/blog/web-development-roadmap-2026-with-ai) to plan your learning path.



\---



\## Step 2 Learn the Basics



Spend one to two months learning fundamentals.



Use



\- YouTube tutorials  

\- Free online courses  

\- Practice projects  



Companies expect willingness to learn not perfection.



\---



\## Step 3 Create a Simple Resume



Include



\- Academic details  

\- Skills learned  

\- Certifications  

\- Projects  

\- College activities  



Keep resume one page.



Clearly mention you are a first year student looking for internship.



\---



\## Step 4 Apply on Internship Platforms



Best platforms in India



\- Internshala  

\- LinkedIn  

\- Indeed  

\- LetsIntern  

\- AngelList  



Search using keywords like internship for freshers or part time internship for students.



Apply daily to at least five internships.

If you want part-time remote experience while studying, these [work from home jobs for students](/blog/work-from-home-jobs-for-students-in-india-2026-without-investment) are another good starting point.



\---



\## Step 5 Message Recruiters on LinkedIn



Search for HR managers or startup founders.



Send a simple message explaining you are a first year student willing to learn and contribute.



Direct outreach increases your chances.



\---



\## Step 6 Prepare for Interview



Common questions



\- Tell me about yourself  

\- Why do you want this internship  

\- What skills do you have  

\- Are you available part time  



Answer confidently and show eagerness to learn.



\---



\## Types of Internships You Can Get



\- Content Writing  

\- Social Media Handling  

\- Campus Ambassador  

\- Data Entry  

\- Graphic Design  

\- Basic Coding  



Stipend may range from 5000 to 12000 per month depending on skills.



\---



\## Common Mistakes to Avoid



\- Applying without learning any skill  

\- Copying resume from internet  

\- Waiting for college placement cell  

\- Giving up after rejection  



Internship search requires consistency.



\---



\## Final Thoughts



Getting internship in first year of college in India is possible.



You need skills consistency and effort.



Start learning today apply regularly and improve step by step.



Students who begin early always stay ahead in placements and career growth.

## What Is PM Internship Scheme?

The **PM Internship Scheme** is an initiative created to help young people gain practical work experience with companies.

The main goal is to reduce the gap between classroom learning and real industry expectations.

Many students complete their education but still feel unsure about:

- How teams communicate
- How projects are handled

This internship scheme helps candidates experience these things directly.
